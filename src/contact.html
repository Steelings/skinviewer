How to trade?

How do I start trading? 1. Log in to the site using your Steam account and paste your trade link if you haven`t done it yet; 2. Select the items you would like to trade from your inventory. Сhoose the items from bot`s inventory. Then click “Trade”‎; 3. Wait until the trade is created. After this, you will get the trade offer with the list of items that you want to trade in Steam; 4. Accept the trade via Steam; 5. Confirm the trade using Steam mobile authenticator and enjoy your new items!
